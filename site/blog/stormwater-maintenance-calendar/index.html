Stormwater Maintenance Calendar for King County Homes

Published June 2, 2024 • Tags: maintenance, gutters, drainage

Spring

Flush cistern lines, inspect overflow piping, and remove winter debris from rain gardens. Redmond and Bellevue homeowners should test sump pumps before spring storms arrive.

Summer

Check gutter fasteners, reseal seams, and refresh mulch around infiltration trenches. Schedule mid-season inspections for Shoreline slopes to prevent erosion.

Fall

Install leaf filters, clear downspout screens, and test cistern shutoff valves ahead of heavy rains. Seattle ZIP codes benefit from monthly gutter checks during leaf drop.

Winter

Monitor cistern levels, clear snow from overflow paths, and inspect rain garden drains for freezing. Kirkland waterfront homes should add sandbags where needed.
