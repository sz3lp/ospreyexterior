Rain Garden Planting Guide for Bellevue & Redmond

Published June 3, 2024 • Tags: landscaping, Bellevue, Redmond

Soil Preparation

Blend compost, sand, and native soil at a 40-40-20 ratio to support infiltration. For Bellevue’s clay pockets, add pumice to enhance drainage.

Plant Palette

Use evergreen huckleberry, sword fern, and sedge for Bellevue shade zones, and red-twig dogwood plus kinnikinnick for sunnier Redmond beds.

Maintenance Tips

Mulch twice per year, flush underdrains each spring, and track plant health in Supabase to document compliance for RainWise inspections.

Coordinate your planting plan with our drainage design services or Bellevue service area page for local support.
