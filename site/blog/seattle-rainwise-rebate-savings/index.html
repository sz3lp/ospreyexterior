RainWise Rebate FAQs

How quickly can I find out if my home is RainWise eligible?

Most Seattle addresses receive an eligibility confirmation within 48 hours. We run GIS checks, confirm roof area, and share expected rebate totals by email.

What does the RainWise rebate cover?

RainWise reimburses rain gardens and cistern systems that meet city design standards. The rebate funds labor, equipment, piping, and overflow management.

Do I need to maintain the system after install?

Yes. SPU requires annual inspection logs. We offer maintenance visits and smart monitoring so you stay compliant without extra stress.

How fast will I receive the rebate check?

Osprey tracks submittals weekly and coordinates with SPU. Most homeowners receive RainWise checks within five weeks of final inspection.
