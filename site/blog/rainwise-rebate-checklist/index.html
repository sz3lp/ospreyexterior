RainWise Rebate Checklist for Seattle ZIP Codes

Published June 1, 2024 • Tags: rebates, compliance, Seattle

1. Confirm Eligibility

Start with the RainWise eligibility map to confirm your Seattle ZIP code qualifies. Homes in 98103, 98107, and 98117 typically meet contributing roof area requirements.

2. Capture Baseline Data

Measure downspout square footage, document existing gutters, and photograph drainage paths. Osprey Exterior uploads these details to Supabase for instant sharing with RainWise reviewers.

3. Submit Application

Complete the City of Seattle application with help from our rebate specialists. We attach hydrology calculations, site plans, and proof of ownership to accelerate approvals.

4. Schedule Installation

Once approved, we install cisterns, gutters, and drainage improvements within three weeks. GA4 tracking monitors milestones and alerts teams when inspections are due.
