How We Retrofit Guards without Disrupting Your Landscape

Our installers document every step in Supabase so you can follow along in real time. The schedule below represents a typical 120 linear foot upgrade.

1. Hydrology & debris audit (Day 1)

We laser-scan roof pitches, photograph downspout outlets, and sample gutter sediment to pinpoint the real clog vectors. A flow model in QGIS projects how atmospheric rivers will load each run.

2. Custom fabrication (Day 3)

Stainless micromesh arrives pre-curved and hemmed to your pitch. Fascia braces and hidden hangers are powder-coated to match your gutters and logged in Supabase inventory.

3. Install & RainWise compliance capture (Day 5)

Crews stage tarps, detach downspouts, and fasten guards at 60° with stainless fasteners. A Supabase checklist triggers RainWise inspection photos and videos for rebate submissions.

4. Storm simulation & handoff (Day 6)

We finish with a simulated 1.5"/hr flush test and thermal imaging to confirm no hidden leaks. Maintenance reminders and inspection documents publish instantly to your Supabase portal.
