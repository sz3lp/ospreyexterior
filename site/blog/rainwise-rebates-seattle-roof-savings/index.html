RainWise FAQ

How fast can I get reimbursed?
RainWise deposits usually arrive within 30 days of final inspection. Osprey submits photo logs and invoices the same day the punch list is cleared.
Do I need to front all project costs?
We stage materials with no-interest scheduling. Your only deposit is a planning retainer applied to the final invoice after rebate release.
How does Osprey track my documents?
We sync files to Supabase, mirroring the homepage workflow. That means your form data persists, analytics log events, and you never duplicate uploads.
What if my ZIP code isn’t currently eligible?
Our team tracks upcoming basin expansions. We prep your documentation so you can submit within hours once Seattle Public Utilities opens your block.
