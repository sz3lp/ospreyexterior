GSI Compliance FAQs

What drives the cost of stormwater compliance in King County?

Capital intensity is dictated by impervious surface area, vault sizing requirements, and the documentation burden for source control. OSI models each factor to minimize excavation while satisfying municipal code.

How does the OSI Compliance Shield reduce O&M liability?

The platform automates inspection schedules, captures live infiltration and turbidity data, and stores auditable logs so your team can demonstrate continuous compliance during EPA or King County reviews.

When should we involve OSI in a GSI compliance plan?

Engage us during schematic design or earlier to value-engineer stormwater vault alternatives and align LID requirements with your permitting timeline.
