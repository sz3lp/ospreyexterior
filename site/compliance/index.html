GSI Compliance FAQs

What drives the cost of stormwater compliance in King County?

Capital intensity is dictated by impervious surface area, vault sizing requirements, and the documentation burden for source control. OSI models each factor to minimize excavation while satisfying municipal code, enabling finance teams to quantify reserves and avoid emergency appropriations.

How does the OSI Compliance Shield reduce O&M liability?

The platform automates inspection schedules, captures live infiltration and turbidity data, and stores auditable logs so your team can demonstrate continuous compliance during EPA or King County reviews. Alerts flag when stormwater vault performance deviates from thresholds so issues are resolved before fines accrue.

When should we involve OSI in a GSI compliance plan?

Engage us during schematic design or earlier to value-engineer stormwater vault alternatives and align LID requirements with your permitting timeline. Early engagement unlocks source control incentives, utility coordination, and inspection bundling that deliver measurable ROI.

Can OSI support post-construction compliance audits?

Yes. Our civil engineers compile sealed inspection packets, reconcile sensor data against permit conditions, and represent your asset during King County or EPA follow-up audits to maintain compliance certifications.
