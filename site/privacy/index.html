RainWise Services Privacy Policy for Seattle Homeowners

We outline how RainWise eligibility requests, Supabase lead submissions, and analytics data are collected, stored, and retained for King County properties.

Information We Collect

When you complete the eligibility form or contact us, we collect your name, address, phone number, email, and rebate status so our team can prepare project proposals and secure incentives.

How We Use Your Data

Submitted details are used to respond to inquiries, verify RainWise eligibility, generate estimates, and maintain compliance documentation. We do not sell or rent personal information.

Analytics & Tracking

Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager record anonymous usage data to improve performance. Event tracking for forms and calls helps us monitor response times and service quality.

Data Storage

Lead form submissions are securely stored in Supabase with role-based access controls. Access is limited to RainWise project managers who need the information to complete your request.

Retention & Location

Project records are retained for up to 24 months on encrypted Supabase tables hosted in U.S. regions to satisfy RainWise audit requirements. Files related to King County permits may be archived longer if municipal guidelines require.

Your Choices

You may request updates or deletion of your information at any time by emailing rainwise@ospreyexterior.com or calling 425 550 1727.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect process or regulatory changes. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Need Assistance?
