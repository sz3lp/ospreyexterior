Our mission

Protecting your home & your transaction

Roof issues aren’t just maintenance problems—they can stall escrow and derail a closing.

Osprey Exterior exists to keep real estate deals on track. We deliver fast roof health inspections, clear bids, and flexible payment options so agents can satisfy Form 35R deadlines and keep buyers, lenders, and underwriters aligned.

While we provide premium exterior care for homeowners, we are uniquely structured to support agents and sellers when the clock is ticking. Expect same-week scheduling, lender-ready documentation, and a team that understands the mechanics of escrow.
