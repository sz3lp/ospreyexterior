Company origin

Founded to solve Puget Sound stormwater challenges

Osprey Exterior was established to address the unique exterior and stormwater management needs of Puget Sound properties.

Osprey Exterior was founded by Luke Preble to provide specialized exterior systems and stormwater management for properties across the Greater Puget Sound region. Recognizing the unique challenges presented by Pacific Northwest weather patterns, soil conditions, and regulatory requirements, the company focuses on RainWise-certified installations, gutter engineering, and compliance documentation.

From initial consultations through installation and long-term maintenance, Osprey Exterior combines technical expertise with deep regional knowledge to deliver systems that perform reliably under local conditions.

Environmental & engineering philosophy

Systems designed for Puget Sound conditions

Our approach integrates environmental responsibility with engineering precision.

Environmental Stewardship

Every system design prioritizes watershed protection, runoff reduction, and long-term environmental impact. We select materials and methods that align with regional sustainability goals while meeting performance requirements.

Engineering Precision

Systems are engineered for specific site conditions: soil composition, drainage patterns, seasonal weather cycles, and existing infrastructure. No generic solutions—each installation addresses the unique characteristics of the property.

Compliance Integration

We design systems that meet or exceed regulatory requirements from the start, reducing inspection delays and ensuring rebate eligibility. Documentation and compliance are built into every project phase.

Puget Sound regional specialization

Deep knowledge of local conditions

Years of field experience across Bellevue, Redmond, Kirkland, Seattle, and Issaquah inform every project.

Soil & Geology

Understanding of Puget Sound soil types, compaction characteristics, and drainage behavior. Systems are designed to work with local geology, not against it.

Weather Patterns

Familiarity with seasonal rainfall patterns, atmospheric river events, and winter storm cycles. Systems are sized and configured for actual weather conditions, not theoretical averages.

Regulatory Landscape

Knowledge of local codes, permit requirements, and rebate programs across multiple jurisdictions. We navigate regulatory complexity so clients don't have to.

Long-term vision

Building resilient properties and healthy watersheds

Our commitment extends beyond individual projects to regional environmental health and property resilience.

Osprey Exterior's long-term vision centers on creating a network of properties across Puget Sound that contribute to watershed health while protecting property value. Through systematic installation of RainWise systems, precision gutter engineering, and compliance documentation, we aim to:

  • Increase the number of properties with effective stormwater management systems
  • Maximize rebate capture for property owners while reducing municipal stormwater infrastructure burden
  • Build a knowledge base of field-tested solutions specific to Puget Sound conditions
  • Establish Osprey Exterior as the trusted authority for exterior systems and stormwater management in the region
  • Contribute to measurable improvements in local watershed health through cumulative system installations

Every project moves us closer to this vision while delivering immediate value to property owners through rebates, compliance, and system reliability.
