Why It Matters

Clogged Gutters Cost You More Than You Think

In the Pacific Northwest, gutters fill fast — and the damage from neglect adds up faster.

🏠 Foundation Damage

Overflowing gutters push water against your foundation, leading to cracks, settling, and costly structural repairs.

🌧️ Roof & Fascia Rot

Standing water in gutters wicks into fascia boards and roof decking, causing rot that spreads fast in Seattle's wet climate.

🐛 Pest Infestations

Debris-filled gutters are ideal nesting for mosquitoes, rodents, and birds — right against your home.

💰 Landscaping Erosion

Uncontrolled water dumps from clogged gutters erode landscaping, flood beds, and undermine walkways.
