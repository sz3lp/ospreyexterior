How it works

Our holiday lighting intake process

From first call to post-season takedown, we keep every step documented and on schedule.

1

Discovery call

We review your goals, timeline, and any HOA requirements while collecting site photos or satellite imagery.

2

On-site measurement

Crew leads map rooflines, trees, and power sources, then capture drone and ground photos for design mockups.

3

Proposal & scheduling

Receive a detailed scope with circuit plans, maintenance schedule, and install window. Approve digitally to secure your date.

4

Installation & documentation

RainWise-certified crews install, photograph, and test every circuit, sharing proof-of-performance in a cloud folder.

5

Maintenance & takedown

We respond to service calls within 36 hours and schedule January takedown with labeled storage to simplify next season.
