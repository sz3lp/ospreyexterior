RainWise Rebates Made Easy: $7.90 per Square Foot Covered.

Qualifying Seattle homeowners can be reimbursed up to $7.90 per square foot of roof area that is managed by RainWise systems. We make eligibility, design, and paperwork effortless.

  1. Check Eligibility: Use the official RainWise basin map to confirm your property qualifies.
  2. Osprey Audit & Design: We complete a whole-property water audit, permaculture plan, and RainWise-ready design.
  3. Installation & Rebate Submission: Our team builds your integrated system and submits every document for reimbursement.
