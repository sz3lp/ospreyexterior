Seattle RainWise & Permaculture FAQ.

These are the top questions homeowners in our service basins ask before signing their master planning agreement.

How fast can I secure RainWise funding once we confirm eligibility?

Most projects move from eligibility confirmation to rebate approval within 4–6 weeks. We pre-fill your documentation, schedule inspections, and submit closeout photos so reimbursements are released quickly.

Can you integrate cisterns without disrupting my historic Seattle façade?

Yes. We design low-profile or concealed cisterns with custom carpentry, paint matches, and landscape screening that protect the architectural integrity of Craftsman, Tudor, and Mid-Century homes.

Do you maintain the system after installation?

Our stewardship packages include seasonal maintenance, cistern cleaning, plant guild refreshes, and remote monitoring upgrades so your investment continues to perform.

Which Seattle neighborhoods qualify for RainWise rebates?

Eligibility shifts by basin, but we regularly serve West Seattle, Ballard, Greenwood, Capitol Hill, Ravenna, Northgate, Beacon Hill, and surrounding neighborhoods. Use the embedded eligibility map or contact us to confirm your address.
