What to expect

Documented five-step process

We plan, install, and maintain each display using the same standards we apply to RainWise projects.

1

Design & circuit planning

We map dimensions, power sources, and controller locations, then deliver a scaled mockup for approval.

2

Material preparation

Custom-cut C9 lines and labeled extension cords ensure every strand fits cleanly without excess slack.

3

Professional installation

Harnessed crews secure every clip, waterproof connections, and run live tests while documenting the layout.

4

In-season maintenance

Storm response and proactive check-ins keep your display shining—replacement bulbs and repairs included.

5

Careful takedown & storage

We label, coil, and store lights in weather-safe bins, ready for next season with minimal rework.

FAQ

Holiday lighting questions answered

Transparent policies keep your install on schedule and your property protected.

Peak weeks fill by mid-October. Booking before October 1 guarantees first-choice windows. Later requests are still accepted with flexible evening or weekday slots.

We supply commercial-grade LEDs and professional clips to ensure safety and longevity. We can integrate existing decor if it meets our electrical standards.

Our crews are on-call throughout the season. Most issues are resolved within 48 hours, and replacement materials are included in every package.

We schedule takedown in January, label every strand, and store lights in weather-safe bins on-site or transport them to our warehouse for off-season storage.
