What’s Happening on Site

  • Flexible fan duct disconnected and venting directly into attic insulation.
  • Uninsulated metal duct sweating during cold weather use cycles.
  • No exterior-rated vent hood or damper at the termination point.
  • Moisture staining and microbial spotting above primary bathroom zone.

We frequently find this issue in Redmond and Overlake bath remodels where fan upgrades were installed without full duct termination updates.

Why This Matters for Escrow, Lending, and Insurance

When a fan vents into the attic, every shower drives warm moisture into a confined space. Inspectors flag this because repeated condensation can lead to roof deck damage, insulation performance loss, and mold growth.

In escrow, this becomes a high-priority repair item because it is mechanical, moisture-related, and usually easy to verify. Quick correction prevents prolonged negotiations and repeated buyer credits.
