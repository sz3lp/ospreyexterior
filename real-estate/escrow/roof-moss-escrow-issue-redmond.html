Overlake and Union Hill properties with shaded tree canopy show the fastest moss rebound and are the most common escrow flags during spring listings.

Why This Matters for Escrow, Lending, and Insurance

A moss-heavy roof can trigger appraisal concerns, repair credits, or underwriting conditions because it signals shortened roof life. Even when no leak is active, buyers and lenders treat visible biological buildup as deferred maintenance risk.

Insurance carriers increasingly request proof of roof cleaning before renewal transfer. If records are missing, the buyer may inherit coverage friction immediately after closing.
