Why This Matters for Escrow, Lending, and Insurance

Fascia rot raises structural and moisture concerns quickly during buyer inspections. Even small sections can imply wider eave deterioration, prompting expanded repair requests and concession pressure.

A documented source correction plus clean replacement work reassures buyers and lenders that the defect is contained, not spreading into hidden roof framing.
