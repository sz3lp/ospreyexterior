# SEO Guide

## Keyword Strategy
- Focus on exterior cleaning, power washing, roof treatments, and gutter services.
- Pair service keywords with local intent modifiers from `geo_targets.json` (e.g., "roof washing in Charleston, SC").
- Maintain primary keyword density between 1.5% and 2.2% within body copy.

## Title and Meta Patterns
- **Title:** `{Primary Keyword} | Osprey Exterior {City}` limited to 60 characters.
- **Meta Description:** 140-155 characters highlighting value proposition, CTA, and service area.
- Include canonical tags referencing the primary service page for duplicate service areas.

## Header Structure
1. H1 for core service or article title.
2. H2 sections for benefits, process, and proof.
3. H3 subsections for FAQs and localized trust signals.

## Schema Markup Rules
- Embed `LocalBusiness` schema with geo coordinates when available.
- Use `Service` schema on services pages referencing `areaServed` fields.
- Publish `BlogPosting` schema on blog posts with author as "Osprey Exterior Team".

## Internal Linking
- Link from blog posts to at least two relevant service pages and one related post.
- Use descriptive anchor text referencing target keyword and city.
- Update footer links to reflect the top-performing pages each quarter.

## Content Refresh Cadence
- Re-evaluate keyword list monthly using the provided prompts.
- Refresh evergreen posts quarterly with new testimonials or case studies.
- Rebuild landing pages annually to align with seasonal demand shifts.
