Privacy policy

How we handle your data

This policy explains how Osprey Exterior collects, stores, and uses information submitted through our website.

Information we collect

We collect contact details submitted through forms (name, address, phone, email, service type) along with analytics metadata such as UTM source and inferred geolocation.

How we use information

Lead data is stored in Supabase to schedule consultations, provide estimates, and coordinate Rainwise and compliance services. We may send service updates or reminders related to your inquiry.

Analytics

We use Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics to understand site usage. Events such as lead_submit, rainwise_lead, and contact_form_submit track form interactions.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared with city agencies for Rainwise and compliance paperwork or with third-party installers supporting your project.

Data retention

Lead data is retained for project lifecycle and compliance recordkeeping. Request deletion by contacting hello@ospreyexterior.com.
