Why custom mesh guards beat national leaf filters

  • Fine stainless mesh: Blocks moss, needles, and fir cones without trapping water on the gutter edge.
  • Aluminum frames: Fastened under shingles and to fascia for better uplift resistance.
  • Rainwise-ready documentation: We capture slope measurements and outlet sizing so rebates stay on track.
  • Serviceable design: Panels lift for maintenance without voiding warranties or damaging fascia boards.

Our guards are field-tested on Bellevue cul-de-sacs, Kirkland waterfront homes, and Redmond tech corridors where evergreen debris is relentless.

Integrating gutters with Rainwise cisterns and compliance audits

We design gutter systems with Rainwise rebate compliance in mind:

  1. Diverter placement: Redirects downspouts into cisterns while maintaining overflow paths to landscaping.
  2. Code documentation: Our compliance team photographs slopes, straps, and discharge points for inspection packets.
  3. Stormwater upgrades: Pair gutters with drainage improvements like French drains to protect foundations.

This integrated approach ensures Rainwise rebates, gutter upgrades, and inspection prep happen on a single schedule—one point of contact, one final walkthrough, and a faster path to reimbursement.
