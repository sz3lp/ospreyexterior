Winter prep checklist for gutters and drainage

Follow these steps to keep water moving away from your home before freezing temperatures arrive.

Step-by-step winter prep

  1. Clear gutters and downspouts: Remove debris, flush lines, and confirm dispersion trenches are open.
  2. Inspect roof and flashing: Check for lifted shingles, cracked sealant, and clogged scuppers.
  3. Test sump pumps and drains: Pour water into basement drains and confirm pumps discharge properly.
  4. Protect exposed pipes: Insulate hose bibs and crawlspace lines.
  5. Stock safety supplies: Keep de-icer, sand, and gloves on hand for icy mornings.
